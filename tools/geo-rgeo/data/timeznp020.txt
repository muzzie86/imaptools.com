  Description:
    Abstract:
      This data set portrays the time zones of the United States, Puerto Rico, 
      and the U.S. Virgin Islands.  Also included are the Greenwich Mean Time 
      offset, and areas in the U.S. where daylight saving time is observed.  
      This is a revised version of the January, 2004 data set.
    Purpose: 
      These data are intended for geographic display and analysis at the 
      national level, and for large regional areas.  The data should be 
      displayed and analyzed at scales appropriate for 1:2,000,000-scale data.  
      No responsibility is assumed by the U.S. Geological Survey in the use of 
      these data.

    Process_Step:
      Process_Description:
        The written descriptions in CFR-TZ were followed to extract features 
        coincident with time zone lines from the National Atlas coverages 
        (ATLAS-CNTY, ATLAS-HY, ATLAS-PL, ATLAS-RR).  The features were merged 
        into a separate Arc/INFO coverage.  Where there were no coincident 
        features, 1:100,000-scale topographic maps (GooseCrrek-100K and 
        Valentine-100K) and county data sets (NWS-CNTY) were used as references 
        to digitize the time zone boundaries.  The NWS-CNTY data set was also 
        used as a reference to define time zones where counties were divided by 
        standard time zone boundaries.

    Process_Step:
      Process_Description:
        Several corrections were made using data obtained from the National
        Weather Service (NWS) website.  Counties that are split between two time 
        zones were edited using the shapefile TZ-SPLIT.  Other corrections were 
        made using the shapefile C_05FE02.  These corrections are:
        >1) addition of a small portion of northwest Cuberson County, TX to the 
        >   Mountain time zone.
        >2) adjustment of  a small portion of the Mountain/Central time zone 
        >   boundary along the 138N and 139N Township line to the Missouri River
        >   just south of Mandan, Morton County, North Dakota.
        >3) addition of Wayne County, KY, Morgan County, TN, Harrison County, 
        >   IN, Floyd County, IN, and Clark County, IN to the Eastern time zone.
        In addition, some boundary adjustments were made using National Atlas 
        counties, ATLAS-CNTY, to ensure alignment with county boundaries.
